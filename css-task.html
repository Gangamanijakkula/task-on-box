MOVIES

MAHARSHI

The film follows Rishi Kumar (Mahesh Babu), a brilliant student from a middle-class family who dreams big and becomes a successful CEO in the United States. Despite his achievements, Rishi realizes that success is incomplete without giving back to society. When he learns about the struggles of his close friend Ravi (Allari Naresh) and the hardships faced by farmers in India, he returns to his roots. The movie highlights his journey of self-discovery, friendship, social responsibility, and farmer's welfare.

THANDEL

The aim of the movie Thandel is to highlight the struggles, sacrifices, and resilience of fishermen who risk their lives at sea to support their families. It brings attention to the real-life issue of fishermen being captured when they unknowingly cross international waters, showing how a small mistake can change lives forever. Through the character of Raju, the film portrays leadership, courage, and a sense of responsibility towards his people, while also exploring the emotional bond between him and Satya, which adds depth to the story. The movie blends romance, patriotism, and human emotions, inspiring viewers to value relationships, understand the hardships of coastal communities, and recognize the importance of standing for oneâ€™s values. Along with powerful music and visuals, the film aims to entertain while spreading awareness about the challenges faced by fishermen and their unyielding spirit.

VISWASAM

The film follows Thookku Durai (Ajith Kumar), a kind-hearted yet fearless chieftain from a village in Theni. He is known for protecting his people and standing up against injustice. However, his aggressive nature creates problems in his personal life, leading to a separation from his wife Niranjana (Nayanthara) and daughter. Years later, when his daughter faces a threat from powerful enemies, Durai reunites with his family and fights to protect them, proving his love and responsibility as a father and husband.

GEETHA GOVINDAM

The film revolves around Vijay Govind (Vijay Deverakonda), a well-mannered and innocent lecturer who dreams of getting married to a traditional, caring woman. He meets Geetha (Rashmika Mandanna) and instantly falls for her. However, due to a misunderstanding caused by an accidental incident during a bus journey, Geetha starts to hate him. The rest of the story shows how Govind tries to win back her trust and love while proving his honesty and genuine character.

HI NANNA

The film tells the heartwarming story of Viraj (Nani), a professional photographer and a single father, who is raising his daughter Mahi (Kiara Khanna). Mahi, curious about her mother whom she has never seen, constantly questions her father. Their lives take a turn when they meet Yashna (Mrunal Thakur), who gradually becomes close to them. The emotional narrative explores love, loss, parenthood, and second chances, revealing hidden truths about Viraj's past and the identity of Mahi's mother.
